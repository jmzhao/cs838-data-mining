Occide{ntal Petroleum Corporation} ({Oxy}) is a multinational oil and gas exploration and production company with operations in the United States, the Middle East, and Latin America.[8] Headquartered in Houston, Texas,[9] it is one of the largest US oil and gas companies based on 2015 year-end market capitalization.[10] Often abbreviated as "{Oxy}", in reference to its ticker symbol, the company is ranked on the Forbes Global 2000[9] and Fortune 500 list.[11] In 2015, it was the 16th largest gas producer[12] and 3rd largest oil producer in Texas.[13]
