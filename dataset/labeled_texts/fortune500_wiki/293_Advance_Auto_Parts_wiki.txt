{Advance Auto Parts}, is headquartered in Roanoke, Virginia and Raleigh, North Carolina,[1] and is now the largest retailer of automotive replacement parts and accessories in the United States[2] by store count after acquiring {General Parts Incorporated} in October 2013. {AAP} was founded in 1932 and had 2015 sales of approximately $9.74 billion. The combined enterprise of {AAP} (including Carquest branded stores) operates over 5,200 company-owned and 1,300 independently owned stores covering every U.S. state, the Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico, and Canada with more than 70,000 employees.
In 2005, {Advance Auto Parts} purchased {AutoPart International, Inc.} which operates 202 stores along the Atlantic Seaboard.[3][4] As of 2014, {Autopart International} still operates as a separate division within {Advance Auto}.
