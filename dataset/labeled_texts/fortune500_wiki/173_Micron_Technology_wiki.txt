{Micron Technology, Inc.} is an American global corporation based in Boise, Idaho which produces many forms of semiconductor devices, including dynamic random-access memory, flash memory, and solid-state drives. Its consumer products are marketed under the brands {Crucial Technology} and {Lexar}. {Micron} and {Intel} together created IM Flash Technologies, which produces NAND flash memory. {Micron} was named one of Thomson Reuters top 100 global innovators in 2012[3] and 2013.[4] {Micron Technology} is also ranked among the Top 5 Semiconductor producing companies in the world.[citation needed]
