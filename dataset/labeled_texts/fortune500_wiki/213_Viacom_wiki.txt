{Viacom, Inc}. (known professionally as {Viacom} (/ˈvaɪəkɒm/ VY-ə-kom) and stylized as {viacom}) is an American media conglomerate with interests primarily in cinema and cable television. It is currently the world's sixth largest broadcasting and cable company in terms of revenue—behind {Comcast}, {The Walt Disney Company}, {Time Warner}, and {CBS Corporation}, but ahead of {21st Century Fox}.
