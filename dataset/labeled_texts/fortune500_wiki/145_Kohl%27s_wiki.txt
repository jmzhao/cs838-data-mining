{The Kohl's Corporation} is an American department store retailing chain. The first {Kohl's} store was a corner grocery store opened in Milwaukee, Wisconsin in 1927 by Maxwell Kohl.[3][4] The company's first department store opened in September 1962.[5] {British American Tobacco Company} took a controlling interest in the company in 1972, and in 1979, the Kohl family left the management of the company. A group of investors purchased the company in 1986 from {British-American Tobacco} and took it public in 1992. {Kohl's} is the second-largest department store by retail sales in the United States.
