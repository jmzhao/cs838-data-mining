{Unum Group} is a Chattanooga, Tennessee-based Fortune 500[2] insurance company formerly known as {UnumProvident}.[3] {Unum Group} was created by the 1999 merger of {Unum Corporation} and {The Provident Companies}[3] and comprises three distinct businesses –  {Unum US}, {Unum UK}, and {Colonial Life}. Its underwriting insurers include {The Paul Revere Life Insurance Company} and {Provident Life and Accident Insurance Company}.[1] {Unum} is the top disability insurer in both the United States and United Kingdom[4] and also offers other insurance products including accident, critical illness and life insurance.[5]
