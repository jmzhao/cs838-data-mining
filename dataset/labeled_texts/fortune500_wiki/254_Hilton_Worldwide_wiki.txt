{Hilton} (formerly {Hilton Hotels Corporation}, and later {Hilton Worldwide Holdings}) is an American multinational hospitality company that manages and franchises a broad portfolio of hotels and resorts. Founded by Conrad Hilton in 1919, the corporation is now led by Christopher J. Nassetta.To be the preeminent global hospitality company - the first choice of guests, team members, and owners alike.
{Hilton Worldwide} is headquartered in Tysons Corner, Virginia. As of October 2016, its portfolio includes 4,820 properties (including timeshare properties) with 788,864 rooms in more than 100 countries and territories[4]". Prior to their December 2013 IPO, {Hilton} was ranked as the 36th largest privately held company in the United States by Forbes.[5]
