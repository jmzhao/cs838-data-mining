AutoNation is the largest automotive retailer in the United States and is the leading provider of new and pre-owned vehicles.[3] AutoNation owns and operates over 310 franchises throughout the United States. The current Chairman, CEO, and President is Mike Jackson, former CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America. The Chief Operations Officer is Bill Berman. It was founded in 1996 by entrepreneur H. Wayne Huizenga.[4] Headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, Florida
