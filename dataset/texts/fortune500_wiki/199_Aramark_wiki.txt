Aramark Corporation, known commonly as Aramark, is an American foodservice, facilities, and uniform services provider to clients in fields including education, healthcare, business, corrections, and leisure. Its core market is North America (United States and Canada) and supplemented by an additional 19 countries, including United Kingdom, Germany, Chile, Ireland and Spain. The company is second in North American food, facilities, and uniform services based on total sales in fiscal year 2015.[1]
The company is headquartered at the Aramark Tower in Center City, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.[2] Aramark's revenues totaled 14.329 billion USD in 2015, and the company was listed as the 21st largest employer on the Fortune 500. Its revenue in 2013 was $13.945 billion, and $14.832 billion in 2014.[1][3]
