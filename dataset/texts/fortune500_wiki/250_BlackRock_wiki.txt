BlackRock, Inc. is an American global investment management corporation based in New York City. Founded in 1988, initially as a risk management and fixed income institutional asset manager, BlackRock is the world's largest asset manager with $5.1 trillion in assets under management.[2]
BlackRock operates globally with 22 investment centers, 70 offices in 30 countries and clients in 100 countries.[5] Due to its power, Blackrock has been called the world's largest shadow bank.[6][7]
