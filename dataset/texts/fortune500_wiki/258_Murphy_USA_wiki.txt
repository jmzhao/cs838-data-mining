Murphy USA is an American corporation operating a chain of retail gas stations, primarily located in proximity to Walmart stores. It was founded as a spin-off of Murphy Oil in 2013.[1][2] In 2014, it was ranked 175 on the Fortune 500.[3]
In the first quarter of 2016, Murphy USA announced a shift in direction with its relationship with Walmart, A new partnership with Core-Mark International will allow Murphy to offer a larger selection of products.[4] and moving to grow their locations independently away from Walmart locations.
Murphy USA operates over 1,300 retail fueling stations in 23 US states. Over 1,090 locations are located within close proximity of Walmart locations, while over 220 locations are stand alone, also known as Murphy Express stores.[5] Murphy USA locations vary from small kiosk locations to larger walk-in formats. Larger locations offer various of products including; fresh coffee, fountain, frozen, fresh food, and beer. Murphy Express locations are typically large format locations (2,000+ square feet) and offer a larger array of products and more fueling lanes.
